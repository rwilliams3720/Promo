How commissions are calculated

            ▼
          

          


            


              

                
Base Rate Calculation

                

                  
Percent type — share × rate%

                  
Flat type — fixed dollar amount per policy, regardless of premium

                  

                    Split sale: primary gets split_ratio of written premium as their share; teammate gets the remainder.

                    Whole sale: agent's share = 100% of written premium.
                  

                

              


              

                
Rate Priority Order

                

                  
1 Subcategory override (if sold subcategory has its own rate)

                  
2 Financial Service rate (if subcategory is tagged FS, no subcat override)

                  
3 Product default rate

                

              


              

                
Minimums & Date Rules

                

                  
Min Premium — written premium must exceed the threshold or the product pays $0 for that sale.

                  
Pay on Issue — when enabled, only sales whose issued date falls in the selected month are counted (instead of sale date).

                

              


              

                
Production Group Thresholds

                

                  
Groups combine policy counts across selected products. All conditions must be met before the group's earned commission is released.

                  
Commission floor — only the amount above the floor pays. E.g. floor $500, earned $800 → pays $300.

                  
Commission cap — maximum commission from a single policy, from this structure, or agent total. Cap is applied after all rates, thresholds, and escalators. E.g. cap $2,000/period, earned $2,400 → pays $2,000.

                  
Requires — a group can require other groups to pass first.

                  
Products not assigned to any group always pay regardless of threshold results.

                

              


            


            

              
Bonus Escalators

              

                The bonus % adds rate points directly to the effective commission rate — it is applied to the group's premium shares, not to the already-calculated commission. This means 12% + 2% escalator behaves exactly like a 14% rate.

                
                  Example: Auto & Fire share = $8,333 @ 12% = $1,000 base commission. Life count = 4 → tier "+2%". Bonus = $8,333 × 2% = $167. Total = $1,167 (same as 14% × $8,333).
